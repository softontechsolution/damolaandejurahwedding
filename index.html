Our Story

Groom & Bride

The Beginning

Our love story is honestly my favorite story to tell and proof that yes you can actually meet your husband right outside your house. It all started on a random warm evening in June 2020. I was outside my house minding my business waiting for a friend and looking cute for no reason when this man appeared walking with his friends.

The moment he saw me he lost all composure. This man started shouting “Who is this beautiful girl?” Not in his head, out loud, in public, with confidence. Before I could even process what was happening he had crossed the road like he was in a movie scene, stood in front of me and demanded my contact.

At that point I was too entertained to say no. I mean how do you refuse that level of boldness? So yes I gave him my number. He called me that same evening and I laughed harder than I had in a long time. Something just felt easy and natural.

A few days later he asked me out to dinner and it was amazing. That was the beginning of our friendship. From then on we basically became walking partners, gist partners, everything partners. Almost every evening you would find us outside walking, talking and slowly figuring each other out.

Then came August 2020. This man already in love asked me to be his girlfriend. And me? I said no. Not because I did not like him but because I was not ready. I thought I was being sensible and mature. He however did not appreciate my maturity. He said he did not want to be just friends and just like that we stopped talking and went our separate ways. Life continued until destiny said “You people are joking right?”

Damola

The Finale

On May 5th 2021 I entered a tricycle on my way to the salon. Guess who entered the same tricycle, Yes Mr Who is this beautiful girl himself. The moment he saw me, he did not even hesitate. He looked at me and said this time I will not let you go, I am going to marry you, No small talk, no how have you been straight to marriage, but this time it hit differently. It was not just bold it was certain and I felt it.

We started talking again deeper and more intentional. It was no longer just vibes and evening walks, it was understanding, growth and real connection. Then on May 30th 2021 he asked me again, Will you be my girlfriend? and this time I said yes and truly the rest is history.

Over the years we have done life together, grown, laughed, cried, celebrated wins, faced challenges and supported each other through everything. Aside from how much we love each other, he made me believe in soulmates because somehow we just fit like we were always meant to find each other even if it took a little drama and one stubborn no at the beginning and our journey together is just about to begin in a brand new chapter.

Now five years later we are getting married on our anniversary and honestly I do not think there is any greater joy than this, Because whatever our souls are made of, his and mine are the same.

Ejurah
